About Statestreet
Retail Group

One of Ghana's emerging premium fashion and lifestyle retail companies — built on the belief that retail is more than selling products.

Our Story

From a Single Store
to a Retail Powerhouse

Statestreet Retail Group is one of Ghana's emerging premium fashion and lifestyle retail companies, operating a growing portfolio of menswear, womenswear, footwear and specialty retail brands.

Founded on the belief that retail is more than selling products, Statestreet is committed to delivering exceptional customer experiences, world-class store environments and timeless fashion solutions for discerning professionals and modern consumers.

The Group operates multiple retail concepts including Boulevard Menswear, Boulevard Women, D'Angelo Clothing, Carbon Shoes, and WoodPeckers, serving customers across Accra through carefully curated collections sourced from leading international brands and manufacturers.

At Statestreet, we are building more than stores — we are building brands, developing retail talent and helping shape the future of premium and luxury retail in West Africa.

Our Journey

2016

The Beginning

Founded with a single brand — Boulevard Menswear — and one store in East Legon, Accra.

Portfolio Expansion

Introduced new brands to cater to diverse fashion preferences and demographics across the city.

Womenswear Launch

Boulevard Women and D'Angelo brought contemporary fashion to Ghana's professional women.

Footwear & Lifestyle

Carbon Shoes joined the portfolio, completing the lifestyle offering.

Today

10+ Store Locations

A network of stores across Dzorwulu, Cantonments, Spintex, Airport, Labone and East Legon.

Purpose & Direction

Our Mission

At Statestreet Retail Group, we are committed to:
Excellence in Fashion: Delivering high-quality apparel and accessories that resonate with our customers' unique styles.
Customer Satisfaction: Offering a superior shopping experience through exceptional service and innovative designs.
Sustainability: Incorporating eco-friendly practices into our operations to promote a greener future.

Our Vision

To be the leading fashion retail company in Ghana by combining global fashion trends with local inspiration to create a distinct and inclusive fashion identity.
