About Boulevard Menswear

Boulevard Menswear is Ghana's leading destination for premium men's fashion, offering a carefully curated selection of suits, shirts, footwear, accessories, and lifestyle essentials sourced from some of Europe's most respected makers.

Founded on the belief that great style is a reflection of character, Boulevard Menswear serves the modern gentleman who values quality, craftsmanship, and timeless elegance. Our collections are thoughtfully selected to meet the needs of executives, entrepreneurs, professionals, and discerning men who understand that how they present themselves matters.

At Boulevard Menswear, we go beyond clothing. We provide a complete menswear experience built around personal style, expert guidance, and exceptional service. From finely tailored suits and premium business wear to contemporary smart-casual collections and luxury footwear, every piece is chosen for its quality, versatility, and enduring appeal.

Our portfolio features distinguished European brands renowned for their heritage, craftsmanship, and attention to detail. Through these partnerships, we bring world-class menswear and footwear to Ghana, making luxury and refinement more accessible to the modern gentleman.

With multiple locations and a team of dedicated style advisors, Boulevard Menswear has become a trusted destination for men seeking confidence, sophistication, and authenticity in the way they dress.

Today, Boulevard Menswear continues to set the standard for men's fashion in Ghana, helping gentlemen build wardrobes that reflect success, individuality, and timeless style.

About Boulevard Women

Boulevard Women is a premium fashion destination dedicated to the modern woman who values elegance, quality, and effortless style.

Located in Accra, Boulevard Women offers a carefully curated selection of luxury fashion, accessories, and lifestyle pieces sourced from respected international fashion houses. From sophisticated workwear and occasion dressing to relaxed luxury and contemporary essentials, every collection is selected with the needs of today's accomplished woman in mind.

At the heart of Boulevard Women is a belief that style should feel personal, confident, and effortless. The brand serves women who appreciate thoughtful craftsmanship, timeless design, and pieces that transition seamlessly across the different moments of life—from business meetings and social occasions to travel and leisure.

Beyond retail, Boulevard Women delivers a personalized shopping experience through its Private Styling Service, helping clients build wardrobes that reflect their lifestyles, aspirations, and individuality. Every visit is designed to feel welcoming, inspiring, and tailored to the customer.

With a distinctive blend of international luxury brands, elevated styling expertise, and a deep understanding of the modern woman's wardrobe needs, Boulevard Women continues to establish itself as one of Ghana's leading destinations for refined contemporary fashion.

Boulevard Women is where global luxury meets effortless living.

About D'Angelo

D’Angelo is a mid-level menswear brand dedicated to serving Ghana’s growing community of professionals with accessible, high-quality wardrobe solutions.

Specializing in professional suits, premium shirts, and business accessories, D’Angelo helps men build wardrobes that support their careers, confidence, and everyday professional lives. Designed for the modern Ghanaian worker, the brand offers a balance of style, quality, and value, making professional dressing both attainable and effortless.

Whether preparing for a job interview, navigating daily office life, attending an important meeting, or celebrating a special occasion, D’Angelo provides versatile wardrobe essentials tailored to the needs of today’s ambitious professional.

More than a clothing retailer, D’Angelo is a wardrobe partner committed to helping professionals look their best at every stage of their journey. Through expert styling guidance and carefully curated collections, the brand empowers clients to dress with confidence and make lasting impressions.

With a strong focus on professional wear, quality craftsmanship, and personalized service, D’Angelo continues to establish itself as a trusted destination for menswear in Ghana.

About Carbon Shoes

Carbon Shoes is the destination for professionals who value quality, style, and practicality. Built for the modern executive, Carbon offers a carefully curated collection of footwear designed to meet the demands of work, business, social occasions, and everyday life.

At Carbon, we believe that great shoes should enhance confidence without requiring a luxury-store budget. Our collections combine timeless design, comfort, durability, and value, making quality executive footwear more accessible to ambitious professionals who are building their careers, businesses, and personal brands.

From formal office shoes and sophisticated loafers to versatile smart-casual styles, every pair is selected to help our customers make a lasting impression wherever they go. Whether you're heading into a boardroom, attending a special event, or navigating your daily routine, Carbon provides footwear that complements your lifestyle and supports your ambitions.

More than a shoe store, Carbon Shoes is committed to helping professionals step forward with confidence. Through quality products, expert guidance, and exceptional customer service, we aim to be the trusted footwear authority for the modern executive.
