Five Brands,
One Vision

A carefully curated portfolio of menswear, womenswear, footwear and lifestyle brands — each with its own character, all united by a commitment to style and quality.

Menswear · Est. 2016

Boulevard Men

Where the Statestreet story began. Boulevard Men is Ghana's leading destination for refined menswear, offering a distinguished blend of Italian tailoring and British-inspired styling.

Designed for the sophisticated gentleman, the brand presents expertly crafted suits, tuxedos, dress shirts, blazers, footwear, and accessories that embody professionalism, confidence, and timeless elegance. Rooted in European craftsmanship and tailored to the lifestyle of today’s successful executive, Boulevard Men is where impeccable quality meets enduring style—helping every man make a lasting impression in fine clothes.

Womenswear · Contemporary

Boulevard Women

A luxury fashion destination for the modern professional woman, offering sophisticated pieces that celebrate confidence, elegance, and success.

Specializing in impeccably tailored power suits, refined office dresses, blazers, pantsuits, and statement accessories, the brand is designed for women who lead with purpose and style. Created for discerning professionals who value quality and timeless fashion, Boulevard Women empowers every woman to make a commanding impression while embracing her individuality and grace.

Clothing · Italian-Inspired

D'Angelo

Delivers smart, stylish menswear designed for the modern working man who values both quality and affordability.

From sharp suits and tailored jackets to versatile shirts, trousers, and occasion wear, the brand offers practical wardrobe solutions for careers, celebrations, and everyday confidence. With a commitment to exceptional value and accessible style, D'Angelo Clothing ensures that looking polished and professional is within reach for every ambitious gentleman. Suits that work—for your career, your events, and your everyday life.

Footwear · Premium

Carbon Shoes

Carbon Shoes is a luxury British-heritage footwear house, created for the modern gentleman who values refinement, precision, and timeless elegance

Rooted in traditional shoemaking craftsmanship and elevated by a modern minimalist aesthetic, the brand embodies quiet confidence through premium materials such as calf leather, Goodyear-welt construction, and meticulous hand-finishing. Designed for successful professionals and aspiring elites who appreciate understated prestige, Carbon Shoes delivers footwear that enhances presence without noise—built to age beautifully, command respect, and reflect enduring ambition. With its philosophy of “Modern Heritage. Quiet Power,” the brand stands as a symbol of cultivated taste and lasting legacy for the discerning gentleman.

Lifestyle · Since 1972

WoodPeckers

Ghana's exclusive destination for premium sneakers and contemporary streetwear, defining the intersection of luxury, culture, and urban style.

Specializing in limited-edition footwear from leading global brands such as Nike, Adidas, and Yeezy, the brand also offers a curated selection of streetwear apparel including graphic tees, hoodies, and essential accessories like caps, socks, and backpacks. Designed for a youthful, trend-driven audience that thrives on hype culture and social expression, Woodpeckers stands at the forefront of Ghana’s sneaker scene—bold, innovative, and unmistakably authentic in its identity.

Shaping the future of premium and luxury retail in West Africa.
