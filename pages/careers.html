Grow Sales

Increase Conversion

Footfall is potential. Conversion is skill. Control the first 90 seconds of client engagement. Ask commercial questions with elegance. Read hesitation and remove friction. Close naturally — without sounding transactional.

Head of Footwear Experience

📍 Carbon Shoes — Palace Mall, Spintex, Accra

Carbon is not a shoe shop. It is a footwear authority in the making — built on heritage construction, masculine elegance, and uncompromising standards. We are not looking for a Store Manager. We are appointing a Head of Footwear Experience.

Apply Now
"You are not managing shelves. You are building a footwear authority brand."

The Mandate

  • Drive 25%+ monthly sales growth
  • Increase conversion through expert fitting and product storytelling
  • Build a 100-client private footwear book within 90 days
  • Sell without discounting
  • Lead and develop a high-performance footwear advisory team
  • Own daily operations, stock integrity and visual standards
  • Present weekly commercial dashboards to the Managing Director

Who We Are Looking For

  • Understands leather, construction, fit and formality
  • Can explain Goodyear welt vs Blake stitch confidently
  • Knows the difference between Oxford, Derby and Monk strap
  • Commercially curious — numbers matter here
  • Has led a team before
  • Sees retail as theatre, not transaction
  • Has the discipline of an operator and the taste of a stylist

What This Is Not

  • This is not a salary-collection role.
  • This is not a passive store position.
  • This is not "open and close shop".

What You Gain

Leadership of a new heritage brand
Performance-based remuneration
Direct executive exposure
Growth within the Statestreet Group
