Five Brands,
One Vision

A carefully curated portfolio of menswear, womenswear, footwear and lifestyle brands — each with its own character, all united by a commitment to style and quality.

Menswear · Est. 2016

Boulevard Men

Where the Statestreet story began. Boulevard Men is the flagship brand of the group — a premium menswear destination built around the vision of redefining how Ghanaian men dress.

Focused on high-quality tailoring, smart-casual essentials and contemporary professional wear, Boulevard Men dresses the modern African man with confidence and distinction. Sourcing from leading international brands, every piece is curated for discerning taste.

Womenswear · Contemporary

Boulevard Women

Contemporary womenswear for the modern professional woman. Boulevard Women delivers curated collections that balance style, sophistication and everyday wearability — from boardroom essentials to weekend elegance.

Designed for Ghana's ambitious, fashion-forward women, the brand champions looks that move seamlessly from professional settings to social occasions without missing a beat.

Clothing · Italian-Inspired

D'Angelo

Sophisticated clothing with Italian-inspired flair. D'Angelo brings a refined European sensibility to West African fashion — offering clothing lines that pair effortless elegance with sharp, modern cuts.

The brand appeals to customers who appreciate the art of dressing well — those for whom clothing is not just utility but a statement of identity, culture and ambition.

Footwear · Premium

Carbon Shoes

Premium footwear curated for those who understand that great style begins from the ground up. Carbon Shoes offers an edited range of men's and women's footwear — from dress shoes to smart-casual sneakers — each selected for quality, craftsmanship and enduring style.

The brand serves customers who invest in shoes that last — not just in durability, but in relevance.

Lifestyle · Since 1972

WoodPeckers

Woodpeckers is Ghana's exclusive destination for premium sneakers and contemporary streetwear, defining the intersection of luxury, culture, and urban style

Specializing in limited-edition footwear from leading global brands such as Nike, Adidas, and Yeezy, the brand also offers a curated selection of streetwear apparel including graphic tees, hoodies, and essential accessories like caps, socks, and backpacks. Designed for a youthful, trend-driven audience that thrives on hype culture and social expression, Woodpeckers stands at the forefront of Ghana’s sneaker scene—bold, innovative, and unmistakably authentic in its identity.

Shaping the future of premium and luxury retail in West Africa.
